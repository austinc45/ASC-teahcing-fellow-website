Naruto
The original show that consists of 220 episodes that is loved by people of all ages.This show is the first time the character Naruto appears. The english cover is voice acted by Maile Flanaganwho also plays Principal Perry in the TV show Lab Rats. This show portrays the experience of Naruto at his young of 13. Here we learn about Naruot's tragic childhood growing up without parents and being hated by the entire hidden leaf village. With the help of his friends and sensei, he was able to grow up with the goal of becoming Hokage later achieved in the sequel Boruto.
